Chapter 2

As Manuela

East Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia

Previous Next

Almost 200 children from Desa Umutnana (Umutnana Village) and Desa As Manuela (As Manuela Village) attends SDN As Manuela (As Manuela elementary school). They go to school every day, in a condition less than ideal: in an old classroom with no doors, broken windows, and old blackboards, and bringing a water-filled jerrycan every day. The children bring water every day to use in the restroom and to water the plants. They almost never go to the restroom, as the restroom is in a very poor condition. Those wanting to read in the library often found the books, placed on a broken bookshelf, lacking.

Our Solution

Solar Chapter Michigan team will work on creating a more conducive learning environment in SDN As Manuela. We plan to renovate the classrooms and library, from repainting the wall, repairing the doors, blackboards, bookshelves, and windows, and also adding books to their library. Solar Chapter UIUC will work on improving sanitation at SDN As Manuela. We plan on building restrooms and an adequate water piping system from our work at Desa Umutnana.
